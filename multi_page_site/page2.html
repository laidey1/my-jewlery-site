About Us

We are a proud minority owned business based in the USA. Our products are ethically mined and handcrafted and shipped directly to you.


Lynea's Jewlery is an online store selling exquisite handmade artisan jewellery and accessories. Lynea is a university graduate with a lot of passion for fine arts. Her work with jewlery started when she developed a passion for imbedding traditional and exotic patterns into wearable pieces. This drove her to create her own specially designed handmade jewlery items. She took this further by enrolling in a special one-on-one jewlery-making course with a world-leading jewlery-maker in France. Her passion for arts did not stop there. Lynea took an interest in creating wire and gemstone jewelery and accessories using fine quality gemstones, crystals and beads.
